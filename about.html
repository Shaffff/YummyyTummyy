About Yummy Tummy

Yummy Tummy is a place where culinary passion meets cultural exploration. Our mission is to bring the world's diverse flavors to your table, creating a delightful journey for your taste buds. Through our carefully curated recipes and food adventures, we invite you to explore the rich tapestry of global cuisine.

Meet Our Team

Nurul is the founder and visionary behind our culinary adventure. With a passion for exploring global cuisines especially Malaysia, she brings creativity and a taste for authenticity to every recipe featured on our platform.

Tengku is our culinary expert, specializing in Korean cuisine. Her expertise and love for cooking are evident in the detailed recipes and video tutorials she creates to make your cooking experience enjoyable and successful.

Hafiz is our Italian culinary maestro. Born and raised in Italy, he brings an authentic touch to our Italian recipes, ensuring that every dish captures the essence of the rich Italian culinary tradition.
