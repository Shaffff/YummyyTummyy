~ AUTHENTIC CUISINE ~

Savor the essence of culinary artistry with our most sought-after authentic cuisines! Explore our most coveted cuisine.

Kimchi Sundubu Jiggae

This spicy sundubu jjigae is made with thinly sliced meat and kimchi. It takes less than 30 minutes, making it perfect for a weeknight meal!

Kimchi Jjim

If you like kimchi jjigae (stew), try this kimchi jjim (braised kimchi). Slow cooked with a small amount of liquid and fatty pork, braised kimchi is tender and packed with flavors.

Dakgogi mandu

This mandu recipe is made with ground beef or pork, onion, cabbage, tofu, and mung bean noodles. The assembled dumplings can be boiled, deep-fried, pan-fried, steamed, or baked in the oven.
