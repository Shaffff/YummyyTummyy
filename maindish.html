~ MAIN DISH ~

Discover irresistible main courses that redefine culinary excellence.

Nasi Lemak Daun Pandan

Taste the twist with Nasi Lemak Daun Pandan—a delightful variation on the classic dish. Imagine coconut-infused rice wrapped in pandan leaves, bringing a unique aroma and flavor to a beloved tradition.

Laksa Penang

Laksa Penang stands out for its harmonious blend of sweet, sour, and spicy elements, making it a must-try for those seeking an authentic and delightful Malaysian culinary experience.

Char Kway Teow

Char Kway Teow is a delicious Malaysian street food—a hot stir-fried noodle dish with bean sprouts and lime. A must-try for those exploring Malaysia's tasty cuisine.
